# Cultural Points Research Summary

Prepared for the Tour de France trip planner culture inbox. Each candidate has its own JSON file in this folder with source links and notes about what each source supports.

## Strongest Fits

- `barcelona-fundacio-joan-miro.json` - Best Montjuic cultural anchor because it is very close to the Stage 1/2 viewing geography and offers indoor time on a hot/crowded race weekend.
- `girona-cathedral-arab-baths-walk.json` - Best Girona short cultural block: high density, walkable, and useful before the Pyrenees transfer.
- `saint-emilion-monolithic-church.json` - Best Saint-Emilion cultural pairing for a wine day; distinctive, guided, and tied to the UNESCO village context.
- `bordeaux-la-cite-du-vin.json` - Best Bordeaux wine-culture museum if the group wants a polished indoor experience.
- `bordeaux-bassins-des-lumieres.json` - Best Bordeaux alternative if the group wants a more contemporary/immersive stop than another wine activity.

## Useful Race-Area Add-Ons

- `barcelona-montjuic-castle.json` - Excellent only if Stage 2 access allows it; race-day closure risk is high.
- `puigcerda-bell-tower-lake.json` - Low-friction Stage 3 town walk once already in Puigcerda; needs local access/parking research.
- `pyrenees-pic-du-midi.json` - Memorable but not short once logistics are included; treat as a clear-weather non-race excursion.
- `pyrenees-chateau-de-mauvezin.json` - Practical transfer-day castle stop in the Hautes-Pyrenees if the final driving route passes nearby.

## Lower-Commitment City Option

- `barcelona-palau-guell.json` - Compact Gaudi/UNESCO stop in central Barcelona; good if larger Gaudi sites are not already planned.
- `paris-galerie-vivienne-covered-passages.json` - Flexible Paris walk with no ticket burden; depends on hotel location and Paris schedule.

## Follow-Up Checks

- Verify July 2026 opening hours and ticket slots for all ticketed sites once final day-by-day timing is set.
- Cross-check Barcelona Montjuic access against official Stage 1 and Stage 2 road/security closures before scheduling Fundacio Joan Miro or Montjuic Castle on race days.
- Cross-check Bordeaux tram and finish-area disruption notices before scheduling La Cite du Vin or Bassins des Lumieres on Stage 7 day.
- Coordinate with food/wine research for Saint-Emilion and Bordeaux pairings so culture stops do not duplicate or fight the meal plan.
