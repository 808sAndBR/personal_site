# Cocktails And Wine Research Summary

Research pass completed for Barcelona, Bordeaux, and Saint-Emilion wine/cocktail candidates. Files in this folder are static JSON candidates only and are ready for planner review.

## Strongest candidates

- **La Cite du Vin / Latitude20, Bordeaux**: best sourced and easiest to schedule. La Cite has official 2026 hours, ticketing, tram access, and a Belvedere tasting; Latitude20 adds a flexible wine-cellar/wine-bar option at the same complex.
- **La Maison du Vin / Ecole du Vin, Saint-Emilion**: strongest Saint-Emilion planning pair. The shop is open year-round with published seasonal hours; the Ecole du Vin has a bookable 11:00-12:30 Saturday tasting format and 25 EUR adult price.
- **Les Cordeliers, Saint-Emilion**: scenic, memorable, and practical inside the village, with official visit/ticketing/bar/boutique support. Verify exact July 2026 days/hours.
- **Le Bar a Vin de Bordeaux**: central Bordeaux wine-bar anchor, but needs current hours and race-week access verification before promotion.

## Barcelona picks

- **Paradiso** and **Sips** are destination cocktail bars with major global recognition. They are promising if the group wants a high-effort cocktail night, but both need current reservations/queue/hours verification.
- **La Vinya del Senyor** is the easier wine-bar alternative in El Born. Good as a low-commitment evening stop if current hours check out.
