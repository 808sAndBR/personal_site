# Coffee Research Summary

## Best Current Shortlist

- **Barcelona / Montjuic:** Syra Coffee Poble Sec is the most practical race-adjacent coffee candidate because Syra publishes daily 08:00-20:00 hours and the cafe sits near the lower Montjuic/Poble-sec approach.
- **Barcelona quality picks:** Three Marks Coffee Ausias and Nomad Coffee Bar are stronger destination coffee choices, but less convenient for Montjuic viewing. Nomad's official page lists the Coffee Bar as closed Saturday, so it needs day matching.
- **Girona:** Syra Coffee Santa Clara is the safest hours-backed pick. Espresso Mafia is the more characterful cycling-culture pick, but needs current hours verification.
- **Bordeaux:** L'Alchimiste, SIP Coffee Bar, and Black List are all source-backed specialty candidates from European Coffee Trip and are central enough for a Stage 7 city day. Hours and race-day access still need verification.
- **Paris:** Cafe Nuances and Telescope are included only as obvious central specialty placeholders because the Paris hotel/walk areas were not obvious from the current itinerary.

## Lower-Confidence Coverage

- **Puigcerda:** Cafè de la Plaça is included as a central fallback caffeine pin, not as verified excellent specialty coffee. Replace it if a better local source appears.
- **Bagneres-de-Luchon / Tourmalet corridor:** Cafe de la Paix is included as a corridor fallback, not a quality-backed specialty pick. It is only useful if the Stage 6 access plan actually passes through Luchon.
- **Saint-Emilion:** I did not find a strong source-backed specialty coffee candidate. Recommendation for now: treat Saint-Emilion as a wine/food stop and get serious coffee in Bordeaux before or after.
