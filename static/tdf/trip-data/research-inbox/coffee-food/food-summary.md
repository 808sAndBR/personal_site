# Food Research Summary

Scope: practical and memorable food options for Girona, Barcelona/Montjuic, Puigcerda/Cerdanya, Pau/Pyrenees, Bordeaux, Saint-Emilion, and Paris. Files are one candidate per JSON under `trip-data/research-inbox/coffee-food/` with `food-` prefixes.

## Best Practical Race-Day Food

- `food-la-boqueria-barcelona.json` - Best Stage 1 Barcelona provisioning candidate. Official Boqueria source supports Monday-Saturday 8:00-20:30 hours, address, trader mix, and market scale. Useful for portable food before Montjuic; not normally useful Sunday.
- `food-halles-de-pau.json` - Best Stage 6 provisioning candidate. Official Halles de Pau sources support morning hours, trader categories, bus stops, and 24/7 underground parking. Use before committing to mountain roads.
- `food-macarons-saint-emilion.json` - Best quick Saint-Emilion snack/souvenir. Official shop source supports daily hours and the historic local macaron specialty.
- `food-bouillon-chartier-paris.json` - Best flexible Paris meal. Official Chartier source supports 365-day opening, continuous 11:30-midnight service, no reservations, and value positioning.

## Best Destination Meals

- `food-normal-girona.json` - Strong Girona dinner candidate from the Roca family, central and reservation-capable. Needs hours verification.
- `food-can-roca-girona.json` - Practical weekday Girona lunch or breakfast-of-the-fork with Roca-family story value. Official site has the clearest hours.
- `food-539-plats-forts-puigcerda.json` - Most distinctive Puigcerda/Cerdanya lead, but confidence is low until official booking, exact address, and hours are verified.
- `food-auberge-des-aryelets-aulon.json` - Best Pyrenees local-products dinner lead for an adjacent night, not a tight Stage 6 race-day meal.
- `food-la-tupina-bordeaux.json` - Best Bordeaux destination meal for Southwest cuisine; official source supports hours and reservations.
- `food-logis-de-la-cadene-saint-emilion.json` - Formal Saint-Emilion starred meal candidate; official source supports history, reservation link, and Michelin-starred positioning.

## Barcelona Notes

- `food-quimet-quimet-barcelona.json` is memorable and near Montjuic/Poble-sec, but official hours are Monday-Friday only, making it a pre-race weekday option rather than Stage 1 or Stage 2 food.
- `food-bar-canete-barcelona.json` is a polished central tapas dinner candidate with an official reservation link. Hours and Sunday availability need verification before relying on it after Stage 2.
